    Use of technology: These days hand calculators often have matrix capabilities, while various computer algebra systems such as SageMath, WolframAlpha, Mathematica and MATLAB are available to help with tedious calculations. In this course, you will be expected to master all the basic concepts and techniques of linear algebra by first solving examples by hand in computationally simple cases. Once these concepts and techniques are understood, however, use of technology is then permitted at all times except on quizzes and exams!
