Homework for Solving Linear Systems


1. Solving Linear Systems

Know how to
  • Compute the Reduced Row Echelon Form (RREF) of a matrix.
  • Use RREF to solve a linear system.
  • Express the solution set in parametric form.
  • Compute the rank of a matrix.
  • Examples:
    • From http://linear.ups.edu/html/section-RREF.html, read Example RREF, Example NRREF, Example SAB, Example SAA, Example SAE, and Sage RREF. Our solutions will be in parametric form. For example, the solution to Example SAA in parametric form is $${\bf x} \ = \ \left[ \begin{array}{c} 3-x_3 \\ 2+x_3 \\ x_3 \\ \end{array} \right] \ = \ \left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 2 \\ 0 \\ \end{array}\right] + x_3 \, \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ \end{array}\right].$$
    • The following is a system of six equations in nine variables. Read through until they give the parametric form of the solution: http://linear.ups.edu/html/archetype-J.html.

2. Homogeneous Systems

Know how to
  • Identify a homogeneous system.
  • Use RREF$(A)$ to solve a homogeneous system.
  • Express the solution set in parametric form.
  • Examples:
