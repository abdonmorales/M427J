Homework for Matrix Inverses


1. Inverses

Know how to
  • Determine if a square matrix is singular or nonsingular.
  • Compute the inverse of a nonsingular matrix.
  • Use the inverse of a nonsingular matrix $A$ to solve $A {\bf x} \,=\, {\bf b}.$
  • Examples:
    • From http://linear.ups.edu/html/section-MISLE.html, read Example MI, Example CMI, and Example CMIAB.
